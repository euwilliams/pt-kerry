SERVICES

How we can help

  • LISTENING

  • Tell me what is going on and how this problem is affecting your life. On average, patients have been to 3-4 practitioners prior to coming in to see me. I want my clinicto be your last stop.

  • EVALUATION

  • I will evaluate your condition with only necessary tests and measures to make a definitive diagnosis. You are already uncomfortable, so I am only going to gather the information necessary to identify the root of your problem.

  • PLANNING

  • Once a diagnosis is made, I will explain the different tools that can be individually combined to treat your condition. Together, we will develop a plan that will meet all your goals.

Treatment Options

Dr. Winge offers three separate options for private, one-on-one treatments; or a hybrid model that combines both in-clinic and Telehealth visits. You and Dr. Winge can discuss which option will be the best for you. Each visit typically lasts one hour and the duration of the treatment is based on how quickly your goals are accomplished.

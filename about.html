ABOUT

I believe that both dysfunction and healing are multifactorial. True recovery is only achieved by treating the body as a whole, and providing management tools for ongoing wellness.

Dr. Winge

Education

Dr. Winge has over 25 years of clinical expertise treating complex musculoskeletal and global chronic pain conditions with a speciality emphasis in pelvic health. She graduated with a B.S. in Physical Therapy from Northeastern University (Boston, MA), and completed her Doctorate in Physical Therapy at Shenandoah University (Winchester, VA.) She is board certified as a Women’s Clinical Specialist through the American Board of Physical Therapy Specialists.

Professional Experience

Dr. Winge holds certifications in Health and Wellness Coaching, and Pilates mat, reformer, and group class instruction, adult and pediatric BLS. She has completed numerous post- graduate education courses including orthopaedic musculoskeletal conditions and treatment, manual therapy, complementary and nutritional medicine, women, men, and pediatric pelvic health, adolescent conditions, pelvic pain and pregnancy, oncology, women’s conditions throughout the lifespan, pharmacology, neurology, diagnostic imaging, and clinical research. Dr. Winge is an active member of the American Board of Physical Therapy and the Section of Pelvic Health.

MY PHILOSOPHY

I believe that both dysfunction and healing are multifactorial. True recovery is only achieved by treating the body as a whole, and providing management tools for ongoing wellness.

MY PROFESSIONAL EXPERIENCE

  • Kerry is a registered Physical Therapist and is a designated clinical specialist in Women’s Health, with certifications in Wellness Counseling and Pilates. She has worked in a variety of settings including sports medicine, home healthcare, in-patient, wound care, and was the Director of Rehabilitation of a multidisciplinary facility.

  • Kerry’s passion for Pelvic Health began after her personal experience of a mismanaged labor resulting in pelvic floor trauma. She witnessed that there were not any resources to assist in her healing. She studied and applied the knowledge that she obtained in addition to developing her own techniques, and was able to heal herself. Her mission is to treat these disorders and transfer her knowledge to empower women, so that they can live their life without pain.

HOBBIES

Kerry lives in Morgantown with her husband, three children and two dogs. In her spare time she enjoys working out, cooking, boating, and traveling.
